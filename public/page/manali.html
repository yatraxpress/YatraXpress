ITINERARY

  1. DAY 1 Delhi to Manali• Depart from Delhi to Manali by bus or car. Overnight journey by bus usually takes around 12-14 hours.
  2. DAY 2
    Arrival and Local Sightseeing
    Hadimba Devi Temple: A unique temple surrounded by cedar forests.
    Manu Temple: Dedicated to Sage Manu, located in Old Manali.
    Mall Road: Explore the local shops, cafes, and restaurants
  3. DAY 3
    Solang Valley and Rohtang Pass
    Head to Solang Valley:Enjoy adventure activities like paragliding, zorbing, and ropeway rides.
    • Visit Rohtang Pass (subject to weather conditions and permits). If Rohtang Pass is inaccessible, consider Atal Tunneland Sissu for similar breathtaking views.
  4. DAY 4
    Manikaran and KasolDrive to Manikaran (approx. 80 km from Manali).
    • Visit the hot springs and the famous Gurudwara Manikaran Sahib

    • Depart from Manali to Delhi by bus or car. Overnight journey back to Delhi
  5. Head to your onward destination
