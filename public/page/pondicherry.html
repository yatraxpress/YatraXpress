OVERVIEW:

ITINERARY

  1. DAY 1 Arrival and Exploring the French QuarterExplore the charming French colonial architecture. Key sites include:
    • Sri Aurobindo Ashram: A peaceful ashram founded by Sri Aurobindo and Mirra Alfassa.
    • Manakula Vinayagar Temple: A historic Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha.
    • French War Memorial: Located on Goubert Avenue, it commemorates soldiers who died during World War I.
    • Notre Dame des Anges: A beautiful church with pastel exteriors
    Promenade Beach: Take a leisurely walk along the Promenade Beach. Enjoy the sea breeze and the view of the Mahatma Gandhi Statue.
  2. DAY 2
    Auroville and Serenity BeachStart early and head to Auroville, about a 30-minute drive from Pondicherry.
    Mantrimandir: Visit the Matrimandir, the spiritual heart of Auroville. Ensure you have prior booking to enter the meditation chamber.
    Serenity Beach: Spend a relaxing afternoon at Serenity Beach. Enjoy activities like surfing, swimming, or simply lounging.
  3. DAY 3
    Local Markets and Departure • Heritage Walk: Take a heritage walk through the Tamil Quarter, known for its traditional homes and vibrant markets.
    Shopping: Visit local markets like Goubert Market for souvenirs, handmade crafts, and textiles
  4. Head to your onward destination
